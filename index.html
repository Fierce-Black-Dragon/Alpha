Why Choose ALPHA?
What is ALPHA?

As we mentioned, ALPHA is voice-controlled personal assistant available for Apple and Andriod users. The idea is that you talk to him as you would a friend and he aims to help you get things done, whether that be making a dinner reservation or sending a message.

What can ALPHA do?

ALPHA can do a lot. He pretty damn clever and sometimes he is quite funny too. If you want to get a giggle out of ALPHA rather than ask him to do something serious. Otherwise,ALPHA can help you when you're out and about, with sports and entertainment information, phone calls and messages, getting organised, tips and tricks and of course, giving you answers.

At a glance, He will read your last email, text your friend to tell them you're running late, shuffle your road trip playlist on Music, let you know what films are playing today, find a table for three in London or call your dad at work. He can also tell you where a good restaurant is nearby, flip a coin, find books by a specific author, set an alarm, give you directions and even set a reminder that will activate only when it recognises that you're in, or have left, a certain location ("pick up dry cleaning", when you leave work, for example).

Works with your favorite stuff, too.
